ABOUT US

Mohammed Islam Trading and Contracting (MITCCO) is a newly established trading company (2020) specialized in supplying of Oil field, Industrial, Construction, Marine, Safety and Personal Protective equipment, as well as spare parts of heavy motorized machinery

Its location in Muscat makes it ideally placed to serve and meet the needs of existing Omani businesses and help new customers in Middle East to extend their operations to the Sultanate.

VISION

To sustain the market confifidence & leadership in general engineering equipments and to further diversify into specialty engineering products and services, to gyrate the company from a “Good Vendor” to a “Great Supplier”.

OBJECTIVE

To actively participate and become a premier player in the rapidly growing market in the middle-east & in the segment where the company provides products and services to the local economies

MISSION

To provide our clients premium quality equipment and products in a competitive price and delivery duration.

VALUES

Customer satisfaction, Honesty, Innovation, Dynamism, Flexibility, Respect, Team Work, Responsibility, Speed, Desire to learn and improve, Efficiency, Optimizing resources, Waste reduction and Day to Day growth.
